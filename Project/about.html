Founded in 2015 on 14th March ,God's Crust sells premium quality pizzas with toppings and ingredients straight from the fields of Italy.

The company got its name from the fact that we wanted to make the customer's pizza experience heavenly as we treat our customers like GOD.

The three founders of this company started this venture when they were in their first year of engineering.
The small venture started in Lovely Proffesional University and has now established as a well known brand in LPU.
